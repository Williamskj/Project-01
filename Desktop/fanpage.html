Bio

Casey Luong, monomynously known as keshi, is a multitalented musician whose unique mix of alternative and indie styles has made him a popular artist online. He credits musicians like John Mayer and Drake Bell for his interest in pursuing music, as well as his grandfather for teaching him guitar with an old Vietnamese music book he had.


keshi has grown his following through online platforms such as Instagram, Spotify, and YouTube and has accumulated over 600 million streams with songs like "2 Soon" and "right here". Before pursuing music full-time, he worked as an oncology nurse for two years at the Texas Medical Center.
